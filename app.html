About Me

My journey into coding began when I first attempted to build a website from scratch. What started as curiosity quickly grew into a passion, leading me to land my first Software Engineer internship through the Microsoft Leap as a Service Programme.

I've since worked as a WordPress Web Designer, customizing WordPress themes to perfectly align with our clients' unique brands, ensuring their websites are both visually appealing and functional.

While I’ve spent most of my time mastering frontend development, my current focus is on strengthening my backend skills, particularly with .NET Core and Entity Framework Core.

When I’m not coding, you’ll probably find me watching K-dramas, playing video games, or enjoying anime with my husband.
