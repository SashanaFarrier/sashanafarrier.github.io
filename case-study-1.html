Project Overview: The Solution

To help E&S Power System Solutions expand their reach and enhance their brand visibility, I designed, built, and hosted their first-ever website. This website serves as a key component of their online marketing strategy, providing potential clients with essential company information, services, and contact options.

Challenges & Solutions

Selecting the Right Hosting & Deployment Setup

Challenge: Since E&S Power System Solutions had no prior website, I needed to choose a cost-effective yet reliable hosting solution that supported .NET Core applications.

Solution: I selected InterServer shared hosting, which supports .NET Core. To ensure smooth deployment, I made sure that everything was configured correctly. I also optimized server response times and caching mechanisms to improve performance within the shared hosting environment.

Optimizing Website Speed on Shared Hosting

Challenge: To ensure fast load times on a shared hosting environment, I needed to reduce file sizes, optimize asset loading, and minimize HTTP requests. Unoptimized scripts and large images could slow down performance and negatively impact user experience.

Solution: I leveraged Webpack to improve the siteâ€™s speed and efficiency by:

  • Minifying and bundling JavaScript, CSS, and HTML to reduce file sizes.
  • Eliminating unused code (Tree Shaking) to keep the final bundle lightweight.
  • Lazy loading assets so that only necessary files load when needed, reducing initial load times.
  • Compressing and optimizing images to maintain high-quality visuals while reducing bandwidth usage.

Results & Impact

By launching this website, E&S Power System Solutions has established a strong online presence for the first time in its 10+ years of operation. The website serves as a valuable tool to attract new customers, showcase services, and improve business credibility.

Additionally, SEO efforts will boost traffic and improve search engine rankings, giving the company a competitive edge in the local solar energy market.

Conclusion

This project was a valuable learning experience, particularly in .NET Core hosting, database integration, and email configuration. Overcoming these challenges reinforced the importance of thorough research, proper configuration, and persistence in troubleshooting.
